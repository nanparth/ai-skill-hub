# Elicitation (shared)

Curated intake for the no-docs path plus standard post-digestion delivery pattern. Loaded by `workflows/guided.md`; delivery pattern also used by `workflows/generation.md`.

## Matter-family intake sets

Pick family, ask its set (3-5 questions). Each set ends with a "what to map" question so selector has an intent. Accept shorthand; skip anything already answered. Fields named = `ExtractionResult` fields the answers populate.

**Litigation** (events, parties, decision_points, concepts, risk_items)
1. Parties and their roles? (plaintiff, defendant, insurer, …)
2. Key dated events, in order?
3. Claims or issues in play?
4. Any if/then legal test to walk (privilege, limitation, jurisdiction)?
5. Show: chronology, claim-element map, privilege tree, or issue-strength matrix?

**Corporate / deal** (entities, ownership_links, phases, tasks, transfers, conditions)
1. Entities and ownership percentages?
2. Parties to the deal? (buyer, seller, lenders, escrow, regulators)
3. Phases or dated milestones (LOI → diligence → signing → closing)?
4. Money movements at closing?
5. Show: ownership structure, deal timeline, funds flow, or approval chain?

**Compliance** (obligations, controls, conditions)
1. The obligations or requirements?
2. Source law or standard?
3. Controls and their owners?
4. Current status per obligation (compliant / gap / in progress)?
5. Show: obligation-to-control map, or gap analysis?

**Employment / HR** (events, investigation_steps, decision_points, states)
1. The employee action or complaint?
2. Dated steps (complaint → investigation → finding → outcome)?
3. Protected class or activity in play?
4. Any decision test (termination risk, accommodation, leave)?
5. Show: investigation timeline, or risk decision tree?

**IP** (ip_assets, states, transitions)
1. Asset type? (patent, trademark, copyright, trade secret)
2. Status and key dates?
3. Jurisdictions?
4. Show: prosecution state machine, or family tree?

**Privacy / data** (data_flows, entities, obligations)
1. Data categories?
2. Systems: source → destination?
3. Transfer mechanism or lawful basis?
4. Show: data-flow map, DSAR workflow, or obligation map?

**Bankruptcy / restructuring** (claim_classes, transfers, events)
1. Claim classes and priority order?
2. Estate assets or distributions?
3. Key dates?
4. Show: priority waterfall, or case timeline?

**Tax** (entities, relationships, transfers)
1. Entities and jurisdictions?
2. Ownership and flows?
3. Transaction steps and their tax consequences?
4. Show: structure map, or funds flow?

**Real estate / construction** (phases, tasks, parties)
1. Phases or milestones?
2. Parties? (buyer, seller, lender, GC, subs)
3. Key dates?
4. Show: deal/closing timeline, or schedule?

**Other** (parties, events, concepts)
1. Who are the parties or entities?
2. What events, steps, or relationships?
3. What should the diagram show?
